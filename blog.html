2-WAY vs 4-WAY

In automated warehouse environments, shuttle systems are a key technology for handling storage and retrieval tasks efficiently. Two of the most common types are 2-way shuttles and 4-way shuttles. While both serve to move inventory within racking systems, they differ significantly in terms of flexibility, scalability, and operational efficiency.
