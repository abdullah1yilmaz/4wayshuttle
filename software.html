The Brain Behind the System

Our intelligent software ecosystem is what transforms mechanical movement into coordinated, autonomous performance. From managing inventory to commanding every shuttle, lift, and conveyor, our WMS and WCS work together to deliver unmatched speed, accuracy, and control.

What is WMS ? (Warehouse Management System)

Our Warehouse Management System (WMS) oversees the complete flow of inventory from inbound receipts to outbound order dispatch. It automatically assigns storage locations, optimizes putaway paths, and manages pick operations to ensure maximum space efficiency and rapid fulfillment.

Seamless ERP integration

Dynamic product slotting

Real-time inventory visibility

Batch and wave picking support

SKU tracking and reporting

What is WCS ? (Warehouse Control System)

The Warehouse Control System (WCS) acts as the operations conductor, translating high-level WMS tasks into specific commands for every shuttle, lift, and conveyor. It ensures efficient routing, prevents congestion, and balances system load dynamically across your entire grid.

Real-time shuttle and lift coordination

Smart traffic management algorithms

Error detection and recovery handling

Load balancing across zones

24/7 autonomous operation
