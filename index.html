Core Components of the 4-Way Shuttle System

4-Way Shuttle Machine

The heart of the system, this autonomous robot moves along both X and Y axes for flexible storage and retrieval at any point in the racking grid.

Vertical Lift (Elevator)

High-speed lifts transfer shuttles and goods vertically between levels, enabling true multi-level operation and continuous material flow.

Conveyor belt

Integrates with the shuttle system for fast, automated transport of pallets or totes to and from inbound/outbound docks, streamlining loading and unloading.

Racking Structure

Modular, high-density racks engineered to support deep storage and rapid shuttle movement, maximizing warehouse cubic efficiency.

The Software

Our powerful WMS and WCS systems coordinate every movement form shuttle roating and lift contol to order tracking and inventory logic-ensuring seamless, fully automated operations.

WMS

Warehouse Management System

Coordinates warehouse logic, product allocation, and task generation. Connected to ERP for seamless order intake and storage optimization.

WCS

Warehouse Control System

Translates high-level tasks into commands for shuttles, lifts, and conveyors. Handles dynamic routing, load balancing, and live coordination across the system.
